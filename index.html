About Us

The Zoomies Pet App is designed to connect pet parents with pet experts, and each other. Our focus on the community side of things allows people to connect with not just their furry friends, but also the experts that can help them every step of the way. From finding nearby vets and supplies to discovering new and interesting information about taking care of their pets, Zoomies is there for them. Plus, by bringing together a wide variety of experts, we can continue to grow the knowledge base of our app so that everyone has access to the best information possible.
